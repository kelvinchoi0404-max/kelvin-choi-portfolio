VALUE / 01

A computer-vision workout assistant designed to guide camera setup, recognize valid repetitions and provide real-time exercise feedback.

02 / CONTEXT

Context

A recognition model only becomes useful when people know where to place the camera, how to enter frame and why a repetition was accepted or rejected.

03 / PROBLEM

Problem

Design a feedback loop that supports full-body positioning and distinguishes valid from incomplete push-up repetitions in real time.

04 / MY ROLE

My Role

Computer-vision product design, interaction prototyping and usability research.
